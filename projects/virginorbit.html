Trajectory Design Analysis and Propulsion Stages Modeling

Projects remains under non-disclosure agreements.

To support the trajectory design team, I consolidated trajectory analysis results with POST into a summary spreadsheet for mission design, mission management, and engineering team leads. I also developed post processing and automated data storage scrips in MATLAB for improved interfacing across engineering teams.

As part of the propulsion team, I modeled the nitrogen gas budget for LauncherOne’s second stage with GFSSP, adding in physics-based components. Additionally, I improved version control best practices by defining user standards in Stash and providing tutorials to the propulsion team.
