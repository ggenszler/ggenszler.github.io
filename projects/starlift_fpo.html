Ephemeris Modeling of Forced Periodic Orbits in Cislunar Space

Forced periodic orbits are a novel type of orbits in the Circular Restricted Three Body Problem (CRTBP). Unlike their unforced counterparts, forced periodic orbits have not been studied extensively. To better understand their potential, I developed an ephemeris dynamics model of cislunar space and augmented existing closed, periodic orbit algorithms to handle low, continuous thrust maneuvers.

This research is currently in preparation for journal submission.
